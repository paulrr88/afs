Who are we?

The Abbeyfield Ferring Society exists to provide very high standards of care for the elderly residents of Ferring and our local area in West Sussex, whether we're providing care at home or at one of our two care homes (Old School House and Cornwell House). Evidence of our high quality care provision include: a favourable Care Quality Commission Report (2013); the Abbeyfield Gold Star Award (2013); and a five star Environmental Health rating (2013).

We are a member of the Abbeyfield group, a network of high quality care homes and care services all across the country, with more than 9,500 people in 720 houses. We believe in the principles of Abbeyfield and all of their requirements - being a part of this organisation provides us with oversight and support for everything that we do.
