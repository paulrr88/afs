Volunteer with Us!

We are always looking for volunteers at Abbeyfield Ferring to enhance the lives of our residents. Volunteers are an important part of the Abbeyfield Ferring team, no matter how much or how little time they can give.

One of the exciting things about helping in our houses is that every day is different! There are so many potential volunteering roles to suit different personalities and skill sets.

Volunteering Roles

We welcome caring people with some time to spare who would like to come and spend time enhancing the lives of our residents. This could include:

  • Chatting to residents
  • Sharing interests and new skills
  • Accompanying on short walks

We are always open to new ideas and suggestions to increase our range of activities.

Become a Trustee

As mentioned on our About Us page, we are run by an Executive Committee consisting of Charity Trustees. Trustees can come from many walks of life, but all share an interest in enhancing the quality of life for older people in a happy supportive environment. They often enjoy the challenge of becoming involved in a small business which is both well integrated into the local community and also interested in improving, changing or finding ways to promote itself.
